About us

Tower Concrete was Founded in 2015

Tower Concrete’s name is synonymous with many notable concrete superstructure projects in the New York Metropolitan Area. Our team of experienced professionals are committed to providing the most outstanding customer service and quality craftsmanship for every project awarded.

New York’s most notable Architects, Builders, and Owners consistently entrust Tower Concrete with their most challenging and prestigious assignments from residential, hotels and commercial projects, or any other concrete structure that is planned.

The principals of Tower Concrete comprise of professional engineers, estimators, draftsman, project, site safety and concrete safety managers and field superintendents exceeding 150 years of combined experience in the Construction Management industry.
